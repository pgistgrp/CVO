LIT Registration Form

Overview and Instructions

Thank you for your interest in the PGIST (Participatory Geographic Information Systems for Transportation) project. In order to determine your eligibility for the study, we need to ask a set of screening questions. This will help us be sure that the people we survey have a diversity of backgrounds and perspectives. If you wish to receive a gift certificate, you must answer each question below, as well as the survey questions that follow.

After you submit this registration form, we will determine your eligibiltly. If you are eligible, we will send you an ID code which you must use to login to the system and respond to the survey. Thank you for participating
