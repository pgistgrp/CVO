Overview and Instructions

Criteria are used to help Evaluate which proposed transportation projects are best suited to address problems with our transportation system. Below, these criteria have been associated with the concern themes discussed in the previous step. Please review these criteria and the associated themes. Economic Vitality
Support the economic vitality of the metropolitan area especially by enabling global competitiveness, productivity, and efficiency

Objectives for this planning factor:

  1. Promotes general economic development
  2. Specifically improves or enhances tourism
  3. Specifically improves or enhances the movement of freight and services
  4. Improves or enhances the movement of workers
  5. Provides new access to jobs and opportunities
  6. Improves the value of residential or nonresidential properties
  7. Enhances welfare to work trips
  8. Improves access to terminal (sea, air, multimodal)
  9. Enhances the ability of the freight system to support product exports/imports
Security
Increase security of the transportation system for motorized and nonmotorized uses.
