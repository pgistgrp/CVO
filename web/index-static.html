Let's Improve Transportation!

If you bus, bike, drive, or walk in the Puget Sound region, this site will help you:

  • Voice concerns about our regional transportation system
  • Learn about proposed improvements
  • Consider the potential impacts of these changes on our communities
  • Discuss with other residents which changes our region should support
  • Cast your vote

This website is currently under construction. You can Learn More about our research project, or contact our research team for more information.

This research is funded by National Science Foundation, Division of Experimental and Integrative Activities, Information Technology Research (ITR) Program, Project Number EIA 0325916, funds managed within the Digital Government Program.
